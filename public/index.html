Biography

In the context of my PhD thesis and the LIFE Healthy Forest project I deal with invasive pathogens causing forest decline in pine plantations in northern Spain. I focus on the usage of statistical and machine learning methods in combination with remote sensing data. I mainly use the statistical programming language R for all kind of analyses and visualization purposes. As an R-enthusiast I actively contribute to several R-packages.
